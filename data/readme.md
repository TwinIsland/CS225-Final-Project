## Weight formula

`W`: weight

`A`: airline number

`D`: route distance

$$
\begin{cases} W = \frac12 (\frac{1}{A}) + \frac12(0.95 + (\frac{D}{4500\cdot D_{max}}\cdot 0.05) &  D > 4275  \\ 
W = \frac12 (\frac{1}{A}) + \frac12(\frac{D}{4500}) , & D \leq 4275 \end{cases}
$$
